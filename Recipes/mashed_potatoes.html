Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Mashed potatoes is a really easy side dish to make any day of the week!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Your favorite kind of cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes in water for 20 min, or until fork goes through
  2. Heat milk and butter in a seperate pan
  3. Drain potatoes and return to pot.
  4. Add milk/butter mixture to potatoes
  5. Blend until smooth and creamy
  6. Season to taste
