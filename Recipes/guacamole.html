Guacamole Recipe

My favorite dip to make is a guacamole. It's suprisingly easy to make and super versatile. Its great to eat with chips, on top of tacos or even on some toast.

Ingredients

  • Avacados
  • Jalapenos
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  2. Mix in jalapenos, cilantro, and garlic.
  3. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate.
